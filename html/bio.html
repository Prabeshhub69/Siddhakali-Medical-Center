About

I'm an entrepreneur passionate about making quality healthcare accessible to everyone. While I'm not a medical professional myself, I recognized a need in our community for a healthcare facility that truly puts people first. My role is to build and manage the infrastructure, operations, and business side of things, so our talented doctors and nurses can focus on what they do best—caring for patients.

Before founding this medical center, I spent years in business management and healthcare administration, learning what it takes to run efficient, patient-centered facilities. These days, you'll find me overseeing daily operations, managing our incredible team of medical professionals, handling finances and partnerships, and constantly looking for ways to improve our services. When I'm not at the center, I'm probably meeting with stakeholders, planning expansion strategies, or spending quality time with my family.
