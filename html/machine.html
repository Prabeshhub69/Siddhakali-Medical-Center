EQUIPMENTS

HPLC

HPLC is a technique used to separate and analyze chemical compounds. It provides highly accurate and detailed results. This equipment is commonly used in pharmaceutical and research laboratories. It helps determine purity and concentration of substances. HPLC works using liquid solvents under high pressure. It is useful for drug testing and quality control. The system is very precise and reliable. It is important for advanced laboratory analysis.

Chemistry Analyzer

A chemistry analyzer is an automated machine used to test blood and body fluids. It measures substances like glucose, cholesterol, enzymes, and electrolytes. The analyzer provides fast and accurate results. It reduces manual work and human error in laboratories. This equipment is widely used in hospitals and diagnostic centers. It helps doctors assess organ functions like liver and kidney health. Results are generated in a short time. It improves efficiency in medical testing.

Drying Oven

A drying oven is used to remove moisture from laboratory materials. It maintains a constant high temperature for drying purposes. Glassware, instruments, and samples are commonly dried using this equipment. It helps prevent contamination caused by moisture. Drying ovens are widely used in laboratories and research centers. They ensure materials are clean and ready for use. The temperature can be adjusted as required. It plays an important role in laboratory preparation.

Electrolyte Analyzer

An electrolyte analyzer is used to measure electrolytes in blood samples. It commonly tests sodium, potassium, and chloride levels. These electrolytes are vital for body functions. The analyzer provides quick and accurate results. It is very useful in emergency and ICU settings. Doctors use the results to monitor hydration and kidney function. The machine requires a small blood sample. It helps in effective patient management.

Hematology Analyzer

A hematology analyzer is used to analyze blood samples. It measures red blood cells, white blood cells, platelets, and hemoglobin. This equipment helps diagnose anemia and infections. It is also useful for detecting blood disorders. The analyzer works quickly and accurately. It reduces manual counting errors. Hematology analyzers are common in clinical laboratories. They play a key role in routine blood testing.

Bacteriological Incubator

A bacteriological incubator is used to grow and maintain bacteria under controlled conditions. It provides a stable temperature that supports microbial growth. This equipment is commonly used in microbiology laboratories. It helps in culturing bacteria from clinical samples. Doctors use the results to identify infections. It is also important for antibiotic sensitivity testing. The incubator ensures accurate and reliable bacterial growth. It is essential for disease diagnosis and research.

X-Ray Machine

An X-ray machine is used to capture images of internal body structures. It is commonly used to examine bones and organs. Doctors use it to detect fractures and infections. The procedure is quick and painless. It uses controlled radiation for imaging. X-ray machines are widely available in hospitals. They help in early diagnosis of medical conditions. It is a basic and essential diagnostic tool.

Serological Water Bath

A serological water bath is used to maintain samples at a constant temperature. It is commonly used in serology and laboratory testing. The water bath heats samples evenly and safely. It is useful for warming blood samples and reagents. This equipment ensures accurate test conditions. Temperature can be adjusted as needed. It provides uniform heat distribution. It is essential for reliable laboratory results.
